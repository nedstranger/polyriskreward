About Me

In 2024, my wife and I found out I’m not our daughter’s biological father. I document this journey, exploring specific topics around open marriage and polyamory. Subscribe to learn how to conquer jealousy and relationship-fear, through detailed real life examples.


Who am I? I'm an English songwriter and unapologetic oversharer. I grew up in Southern England, moved to Cambridge to study law, then to London to explore the music scene, where I met Anya. Together we moved to Vienna in 2022 where we've lived together ever since.
