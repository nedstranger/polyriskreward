About the book

A relationship memoir and philosophical reckoning, Poly Risk Reward looks at what happened when ideas about openness, honesty, jealousy, possession, and freedom stopped being abstract and became painfully real.

Personal

A first-hand account of love, insecurity, hope, fatherhood, and the emotional fallout of confronting uncertainty at the deepest possible level.

Philosophical

An exploration of monogamy, non-monogamy, truth, freedom, and whether our ideals can survive contact with real life.

Uncomfortable

Not a manifesto and not a tidy self-help book — more an honest attempt to think clearly in the middle of fear, longing, jealousy, and love.

About the author

I'm a songwriter, storyteller and unapologetic oversharer. After graduating from Cambridge with a Law Masters, I moved to London to seek my fortune as a musician, realising too late that I'd done the law-music switch the wrong way round.

Nonetheless, with my bandmate and best friend Vedantha Kumar, I grew our indie-folk group August and After to millions of listeners across the world with features on Official Spotify playlists, BBC Radio, The Independent, Kerrang! and Songwriting Magazine.

In 2018 I met Anya, a polyamorous, Latvian-born painter and together we moved around Europe before settling in Vienna, where I launched my solo music act Ned Stranger and explored the risks and rewards of open marriage, inadvertently living the story that would become this book.
