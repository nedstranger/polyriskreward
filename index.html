About the book

A relationship memoir and philosophical reckoning, Poly Risk Reward looks at what happened when ideas about openness, honesty, jealousy, possession, and freedom stopped being abstract and became painfully real.

Poly Risk Reward is a relationship memoir and philosophical reckoning about what happened when ideas about openness, honesty, jealousy, possession, and freedom stopped being abstract and became painfully real. It explores the emotional and moral fallout of uncertainty in love, fatherhood, and identity, and asks what remains when intimate ideals collide with fear, desire, and the need for truth.

Part personal story and part attempt to think clearly under pressure, the book does not offer a neat manifesto or a tidy set of answers. Instead, it tries to stay with the real questions: what love asks of us, what freedom costs, and whether honesty can survive where the stakes are highest.
